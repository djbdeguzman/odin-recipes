How to make a Leche Flan

Description

(in Filipino cuisine) a custard dessert made with condensed milk and egg yolks and topped with caramel.

Ingredients

  • 10 pieces eggs
  • 1 can condensed milk (14 oz)
  • 1 cup fresh milk or evaporated milk
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
