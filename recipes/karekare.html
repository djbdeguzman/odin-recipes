How to cook Kare-kare?

Description

(in Filipino cuisine) a stew of meat (especially oxtail and tripe) and vegetables, cooked in a thick, peanut-based sauce and colored with annatto.

Ingredients

  • 4 lbs. beef tongue
  • ¾ cups peanut
  • ¾ cup almonds
  • 2 bunches bok choy
  • 1 Chinese eggplant
  • 12 string beans cut in 2-inch pieces
  • 1 onion minced
  • 5 cloves garlic chopped
  • 2 quarts water
  • 32 ounces beef broth
  • 4 tablespoons annatto oil
  • Salt to taste
